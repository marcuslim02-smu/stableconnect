GENIUS Act (Guiding and Establishing National Innovation for U.S. Stablecoins): Federal framework for payment stablecoin issuers requiring 1:1 reserve backing, regulated issuer status, prohibition on interest (direct), monthly reserve disclosure, and redemption rights. StableConnect uses only GENIUS-approved stablecoin instruments.
